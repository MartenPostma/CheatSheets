Model Validation

Information

After a model fit, it is important to validate your model.

First, you have to determine a metric to represent predictive accuracy.

It's problematic to validate your model on the data you trained it with, called in-sample scores.

The most common way is to split your data into two parts

  • training data: the data used to train the model
  • validation data: the data used to validate the model
Usage

sklearn can perform the train and validate split using

from sklearn.model_selection import train_test_split
